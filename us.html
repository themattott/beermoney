How We Work

One room.
No telephone game.

01

Write it

The script comes from the people who’ll defend it on set. No briefs handed down to junior copy.

02

Direct it

The director was in the room when the joke was written. The take you want is the one that was always coming.

03

Cut it

Comedy lives or dies in the edit. The people who wrote and shot the joke are the ones finding the frame it lands on.

04

Defend it

When a note comes in that would flatten the spot, somebody at this number picks up and pushes back. Politely. Mostly.
